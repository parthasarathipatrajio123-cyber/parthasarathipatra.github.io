About Me

I am a PhD research fellow at the Department of English, Jadavpur University, working on South Asian Literature. I am a first-generation learner and a person with disabilities affected with cerebral palsy. Having grown up in the under-privileged town of Bankura, I have faced innumerable challenges on my journey to academia.

My academic and personal journey is driven by a commitment to humanities research, disability rights advocacy, and understanding the geopolitical history of South Asia. I believe that through scholarship and social engagement, we can reshape how society understands and includes persons with disabilities.

"I can't change the way I look, but I can aspire to change the way the world looks at me."
