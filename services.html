Services

I offer a range of academic, editorial, advocacy, and financial analysis services. Whether you are a student, researcher, institution, or a retail investor seeking informed market insights, I am happy to collaborate and assist. Feel free to get in touch.

Academic, Research, Editorial & Advocacy Related Services

📚

Academic Tutoring & Teaching

One-on-one or group tutoring in English Literature, Humanities, and communication skills for undergraduate and postgraduate students. Teaching TCESS topics to STEM students at Jadavpur University since 2023.

🔍

Research Consultation

Guidance and consultation for research in South Asian Literature, postcolonial theory, trauma studies, necropolitics, Kashmiri narratives, disability studies, and the geopolitical history of South Asia.

✍

Editing & Proofreading

Professional editing and proofreading of academic papers, theses, book chapters, conference papers, and research articles in English. Backed by a Post-Graduate Certificate in Editing and Publishing from SCTR, Jadavpur University.

🌐

Translation (Bengali ↔ English)

Translation of academic and literary texts between Bengali and English. Certified with a UGC-approved Certificate Course on Translation as a Skill from CENTIL, Jadavpur University (2022).

📝

Content & Digital Writing

Writing and editing content for academic blogs, newsletters, digital platforms, and editorial projects with a focus on humanities, social issues, and disability rights narratives.

✊

Disability Rights Advocacy

Awareness sessions, panel discussions, consultations, and advocacy support for persons with disabilities in academic and institutional settings, drawing on lived experience and CP Rise Leadership training by EnAble India.

Equity Market & Portfolio Analysis

Offering accessible, research-driven equity market insights and portfolio guidance tailored specifically for retail investors — helping individuals make informed, confident, and well-considered investment decisions in the Indian and global equity markets.

📈

Stock Portfolio Analysis

Review and analysis of your existing stock portfolio — identifying strengths, gaps, concentration risks, and rebalancing opportunities to align with your financial goals.

📊

Equity Research & Stock Screening

Fundamental and thematic research on individual stocks and sectors — helping retail investors identify quality companies at the right valuations before making investment decisions.

🏹

Market Trends & Sector Insights

Periodic analysis of broader market trends, sectoral movements, and macroeconomic factors that affect retail investor portfolios in the Indian equity market (NSE/BSE).

💰

Retail Investor Education

Simple, jargon-free sessions and write-ups explaining equity investing concepts — from understanding P/E ratios to building a long-term SIP-based portfolio — designed for first-time and early-stage investors.

⚖

Risk Assessment & Asset Allocation

Helping retail investors understand their own risk appetite and build a balanced asset allocation strategy across equity, debt, and other instruments suited to their financial horizon.

🏆
Common Objective Across All Services

Mentorship for First-Generation Learners

Underlying every service I offer — whether academic, editorial, or financial — is a deep commitment to guiding students and individuals from underprivileged and first-generation backgrounds. I believe that access to quality knowledge, research skills, and informed financial decision-making should never be limited by one's socioeconomic origin. Through mentorship, I aim to help people navigate higher education, fellowship applications, NET/GATE preparation, research career pathways, and sound investment choices — with confidence and clarity.
