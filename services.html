Services

I offer a range of academic, editorial, and advocacy services rooted in my training in humanities research, teaching, editing, translation, and disability rights. Whether you are a student, researcher, institution, or organisation, I am happy to collaborate and assist. Feel free to get in touch.

Academic & Research Services

📚

Academic Tutoring & Teaching

One-on-one or group tutoring in English Literature, Humanities, and communication skills for undergraduate and postgraduate students. I have been teaching TCESS topics to STEM students at Jadavpur University since 2023.

🔍

Research Consultation

Guidance and consultation for research in South Asian Literature, postcolonial theory, trauma studies, necropolitics, Kashmiri narratives, disability studies, and the geopolitical history of South Asia.

📖

Thesis & Dissertation Guidance

Assistance in structuring arguments, framing research questions, reviewing drafts, and strengthening academic writing for M.A., M.Phil, and PhD students in English and related humanities disciplines.

Editorial & Writing Services

✍

Editing & Proofreading

Professional editing and proofreading of academic papers, theses, book chapters, conference papers, and research articles in English. Backed by a Post-Graduate Certificate in Editing and Publishing from SCTR, Jadavpur University.

🌐

Translation (Bengali ↔ English)

Translation of academic and literary texts between Bengali and English. Certified with a UGC-approved Certificate Course on Translation as a Skill from CENTIL, Jadavpur University (2022).

📝

Content & Digital Writing

Writing and editing content for academic blogs, newsletters, digital platforms, and editorial projects with a focus on humanities, social issues, and disability rights narratives.

Advocacy & Outreach

✊

Disability Rights Advocacy

Awareness sessions, panel discussions, consultations, and advocacy support for persons with disabilities in academic and institutional settings. Drawing on lived experience and formal leadership training through the CP Rise Programme by EnAble India.

🏆

Mentorship for First-Generation Learners

Guidance and mentorship for students from under-privileged backgrounds who are navigating higher education, fellowship applications, NET/GATE preparation, and research career pathways.

🗣

Guest Talks & Seminars

Available for invited talks, panel presentations, and seminar participation on topics including South Asian Literature, Kashmir conflict narratives, disability studies, and postcolonial theory.
